Marketplace Fee Calculator

Calculate marketplace fees, selling costs, net profit, profit margin, and ROI before listing your products.

Enter your values, then tap Calculate.

How to Use

Step 1: Select Your Marketplace

Choose the marketplace where you plan to sell your product. You can also select Custom Fee if you want to enter your own marketplace fee percentage.

Step 2: Select Your Currency

Choose the currency you want to use for the calculation.

Step 3: Enter the Selling Price

Enter the price your customer will pay for one item.

Step 4: Enter the Product Cost

Enter your total cost for one product before marketplace fees.

Step 5: Review the Marketplace Fee

The calculator automatically fills the fee for supported marketplaces. You can change it manually if needed.

Step 6: Tap Calculate

The calculator instantly shows your marketplace fee, net revenue, profit, profit margin, and ROI.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a marketplace fee?

A marketplace fee is the commission charged by an online marketplace for every successful sale.

Why is net revenue different from selling price?

Your marketplace deducts its commission before paying you, so your net revenue is lower than your selling price.

What is profit margin?

Profit margin shows what percentage of your selling price becomes profit after marketplace fees and product costs.

What is ROI?

Return on Investment (ROI) measures how much profit you earn compared to the cost of your product.

Can I use my own fee percentage?

Yes. Select Custom Fee and enter any marketplace commission percentage.

Formula

Marketplace Fee

Marketplace Fee = Selling Price × Fee Rate

Net Revenue

Net Revenue = Selling Price − Marketplace Fee

Profit

Profit = Net Revenue − Product Cost

Profit Margin

Profit Margin = (Profit ÷ Selling Price) × 100

Return on Investment (ROI)

ROI = (Profit ÷ Product Cost) × 100
