Import Cost Calculator

Estimate the total landed cost of imported products by including product cost, shipping, customs duty, VAT, and other fees.

Enter your import details, then tap Calculate.

How to Use

Step 1: Select Your Currency

Choose the currency you want to use for your import cost calculation.

Step 2: Enter Product Cost

Input the total purchase cost of your products from the supplier.

Step 3: Enter Shipping Cost

Include international shipping, freight, or courier costs.

Step 4: Enter Taxes and Fees

Enter the customs duty percentage, VAT percentage, and any additional import fees.

Step 5: Enter Quantity and Selling Price

Input the number of items you imported and your planned selling price per item.

Step 6: Tap Calculate

The calculator will instantly show your total import cost, landed cost per item, revenue, profit, profit margin, and ROI.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an import cost?

Import cost is the total amount you spend to bring products into your country. It usually includes the product price, shipping, customs duties, taxes, and other related fees.

What is landed cost?

Landed cost is the complete cost of getting a product to your destination. Knowing your landed cost helps you price your products accurately and avoid unexpected losses.

Why should I calculate cost per item?

Cost per item shows how much each imported product actually costs after all expenses are included. This makes it easier to set profitable selling prices.

What is ROI?

ROI (Return on Investment) measures how much profit you earn compared to your total investment. A higher ROI generally indicates a more profitable business.

Can I use this calculator for bulk imports?

Yes. This calculator works for both small and large shipments, making it useful for online sellers, importers, resellers, and small businesses.

Formula

Total Import Cost

Total Import Cost = Product Cost + Shipping Cost + Customs Duty + VAT + Other Fees

Cost per Item

Cost per Item = Total Import Cost ÷ Quantity

Total Revenue

Revenue = Selling Price × Quantity

Total Profit

Profit = Revenue − Total Import Cost

Profit Margin

Profit Margin = (Profit ÷ Revenue) × 100

Return on Investment (ROI)

ROI = (Profit ÷ Total Import Cost) × 100
