Import Cost Calculator

Calculate the total landed cost of imported products including shipping, customs duties, taxes, and additional fees.

Enter all import costs, then tap Calculate.

How to Use

Step 1: Enter Product Cost

Enter the total purchase price of your products.

Step 2: Enter Shipping Cost

Enter the amount you paid for shipping.

Step 3: Enter Taxes and Fees

Include import tax, customs fees, and any additional charges.

Step 4: Enter Quantity

Enter the total number of items being imported.

Step 5: Tap Calculate

The calculator will display your total landed cost and the cost per item.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is landed cost?

Landed cost is the total cost of importing products, including the purchase price, shipping, customs duties, taxes, and other fees.

Why calculate cost per item?

Knowing the cost per item helps you price products correctly and calculate your profit margin.

Can I leave some fees blank?

Yes. Any fee left blank will automatically be treated as zero.

Formula

Total Import Cost = Product Cost + Shipping Cost + Import Tax + Customs Fee + Other Fees

Cost Per Item = Total Import Cost ÷ Quantity
