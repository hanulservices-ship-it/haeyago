VAT Calculator

Add VAT, remove VAT, or calculate the VAT amount in seconds.

Choose a calculation type, enter the values, then tap Calculate.

How to Use

Add VAT

Enter the original price before VAT. The calculator will show the VAT amount and the final price including VAT.

Remove VAT

Enter the total amount that already includes VAT. The calculator will determine the original amount and the VAT portion.

VAT Amount Only

Enter any amount to calculate only the VAT value without adding or removing it.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is VAT?

Value-Added Tax (VAT) is a consumption tax added to goods and services. Different countries use different VAT rates.

Can I change the VAT rate?

Yes. Simply replace the default 12% with your country's VAT or tax rate.

Does this work outside the Philippines?

Yes. You can use any VAT or sales tax percentage.

Can I use decimal VAT rates?

Yes. Decimal values such as 7.5% or 15.25% are supported.
