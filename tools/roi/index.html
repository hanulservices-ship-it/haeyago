ROI Calculator

Calculate your Return on Investment (ROI), profit, and investment performance based on your investment and revenue.

Enter your Investment and Revenue, then tap Calculate.

How to Use

Step 1: Enter Your Investment

Enter the total amount you invested in your project, business, or product.

Step 2: Enter Your Revenue

Enter the total revenue or income generated from that investment.

Step 3: Tap Calculate

The calculator will display your Profit and Return on Investment (ROI) percentage.

Example

If you invested ₱10,000 and earned ₱13,000, your Profit is ₱3,000 and your ROI is 30%.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ROI?

ROI stands for Return on Investment. It measures how much profit you earn compared to the amount you invested.

What does a higher ROI mean?

A higher ROI generally means your investment generated a greater return relative to its cost.

Can ROI be negative?

Yes. If your revenue is lower than your investment, your ROI will be negative, indicating a loss.

Who can use this calculator?

Business owners, online sellers, freelancers, investors, and anyone who wants to evaluate the performance of an investment.

Formula

Profit

Profit = Revenue − Investment

ROI (%)

ROI = (Profit ÷ Investment) × 100

Example

Investment = ₱10,000
Revenue = ₱13,000

Profit = ₱3,000
ROI = 30.00%
