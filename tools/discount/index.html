Discount Calculator

Calculate the discount amount, final price, and total savings in seconds.

Enter the Original Price and Discount Percentage, then tap Calculate.

How to Use

Step 1: Enter the Original Price

Enter the item's price before any discounts are applied.

Step 2: Enter the Discount Percentage

Input the discount percentage offered by the store or seller.

Step 3: Tap Calculate

The calculator will instantly show your discount amount, final price, and total money saved.

Example

If the Original Price is ₱2,000 and the Discount is 25%, you save ₱500 and pay only ₱1,500.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a discount?

A discount is a reduction from the original selling price of a product or service.

How is the discount amount calculated?

Multiply the original price by the discount percentage and divide by 100.

Can I use decimal percentages?

Yes. Values like 12.5% or 7.75% are supported.

Does this work for any currency?

Yes. The calculator works for any currency. The currency selector only changes the displayed symbol.

Formula

Discount Amount

Discount = Original Price × (Discount % ÷ 100)

Final Price

Final Price = Original Price − Discount

Example

Original Price = ₱2,000
Discount = 25%

Discount Amount = ₱500
Final Price = ₱1,500
