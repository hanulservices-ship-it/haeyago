Shipping Cost Calculator

Estimate shipping costs using your package weight and shipping rate.

Enter the package weight and shipping rate, then tap Calculate.

How to Use

Step 1: Select Your Currency

Choose the currency you want to display for the shipping cost.

Step 2: Enter Package Weight

Enter the total weight of your package in kilograms.

Step 3: Enter Shipping Rate per kg

Enter the shipping cost charged for every kilogram.

Step 4: Tap Calculate

The calculator will estimate the total shipping cost instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is shipping cost calculated?

The calculator multiplies the package weight by the shipping rate per kilogram.

Can I use decimal weights?

Yes. You can enter values such as 0.25 kg, 1.5 kg, or 2.75 kg.

Does this include volumetric weight?

No. This version only calculates based on actual weight. Volumetric weight will be added in a future update.

Can I use any currency?

Yes. The currency selector changes the displayed symbol only.

Formula

Total Shipping Cost

Shipping Cost = Package Weight × Shipping Rate per kg

Example

Weight = 3 kg
Rate = ₱180/kg

Shipping Cost = ₱540
