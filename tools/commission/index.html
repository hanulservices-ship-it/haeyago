Commission Calculator

Calculate your commission, net sales, and earnings based on your sales amount and commission rate.

Enter your sales amount and commission rate, then tap Calculate.

How to Use

Step 1: Enter Sales Amount

Enter the total value of the sale or transaction.

Step 2: Enter Commission Rate

Enter the agreed commission percentage.

Step 3: Tap Calculate

The calculator will display the commission amount and the remaining sales amount after commission.

Example

If your sales amount is ₱10,000 and your commission rate is 10%, your commission is ₱1,000 and the remaining sales amount is ₱9,000.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a commission?

A commission is a payment earned based on a percentage of a sale or transaction.

Who can use this calculator?

Sales representatives, freelancers, affiliate marketers, insurance agents, real estate agents, and business owners.

Can I use decimal percentages?

Yes. You can enter commission rates such as 7.5% or 12.25%.

Does it support different currencies?

Yes. The currency selector only changes the displayed symbol.

Formula

Commission

Commission = Sales × (Commission Rate ÷ 100)

Net Sales

Net Sales = Sales − Commission

Example

Sales = ₱10,000
Commission Rate = 10%

Commission = ₱1,000
Net Sales = ₱9,000
