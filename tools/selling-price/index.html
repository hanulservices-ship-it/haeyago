Selling Price Calculator

Calculate the ideal selling price based on your cost price and desired markup percentage.

Enter your Cost Price and Desired Markup, then tap Calculate.

How to Use

Step 1: Enter Your Cost Price

Enter the total cost of your product or service.

Step 2: Enter Your Desired Markup

Type the markup percentage you want to earn on each sale.

Step 3: Tap Calculate

The calculator will determine the Selling Price and expected Profit.

Example

If your Cost Price is ₱100 and your Desired Markup is 30%, your Selling Price will be ₱130 and your Profit will be ₱30.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the selling price calculated?

The calculator adds your desired markup percentage to your cost price to determine the selling price.

Can I use decimal markup values?

Yes. You can enter whole numbers or decimal percentages such as 12.5%.

Why should I use a markup percentage?

Using a consistent markup helps you price products fairly while maintaining your target profit.

Can I use this for services?

Yes. The calculator works for products, services, freelance projects, and other business offerings.

Formula

Selling Price

Selling Price = Cost Price × (1 + Markup ÷ 100)

Profit

Profit = Selling Price − Cost Price

Example

Cost Price = ₱100
Desired Markup = 30%

Selling Price = ₱130
Profit = ₱30
