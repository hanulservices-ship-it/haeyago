📦 Volumetric Weight Calculator

Calculate the volumetric (dimensional) weight, actual weight, and chargeable weight for parcels, courier shipments, air cargo, and freight.

Calculator

Enter your package dimensions and weight, then tap Calculate.

How to Use

Step 1: Enter Your Cost Price

Enter the total cost of your product or service. This includes manufacturing costs, purchase price, shipping, or any other expenses directly related to the item.

Step 2: Enter Your Selling Price

Type the price you plan to charge your customer. Make sure the selling price is higher than your cost price if you want to earn a profit.

Step 3: Tap Calculate

The calculator will instantly display your Profit, Profit Margin (%), and Markup (%) based on the values you entered.

Example

If your Cost Price is ₱100 and your Selling Price is ₱150, your Profit is ₱50, your Profit Margin is 33.33%, and your Markup is 50.00%.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is profit margin?

Profit margin is the percentage of your selling price that remains as profit after deducting the cost. It helps measure how profitable each sale is.

What is the difference between profit margin and markup?

Profit margin is based on the selling price, while markup is based on the cost price. They are related but are not the same percentage.

Can I use this calculator for services?

Yes. You can use it for physical products, digital products, freelance work, consulting, and other services.

Does this calculator support different currencies?

Yes. The currency selector changes the displayed currency symbol only. The calculation works the same regardless of the currency you choose.

Formula

Profit

Profit = Selling Price − Cost Price

Profit Margin (%)

Profit Margin = (Profit ÷ Selling Price) × 100

Markup (%)

Markup = (Profit ÷ Cost Price) × 100

Example

Selling Price = ₱150

Profit = ₱50
Profit Margin = 33.33%
Markup = 50.00%

💡 Tips

  • Couriers charge whichever is higher: Actual Weight or Volumetric Weight.
  • Using a smaller box can reduce your shipping cost.
  • Remove unnecessary packaging to lower dimensional weight.
  • Different shipping companies may use different divisors.

How to Use

  1. Enter the package Length, Width, and Height.
  2. Select the dimension unit (cm or inches).
  3. Enter the package's actual weight.
  4. Select the weight unit.
  5. Select the shipping divisor or enter a custom one.
  6. Tap Calculate.
  7. The calculator will display the Volumetric Weight, Actual Weight, and Chargeable Weight.

Formula

Volumetric Weight

Length × Width × Height ÷ Shipping Divisor

Chargeable Weight

Greater of:

  • Actual Weight
  • Volumetric Weight

Supported Divisors

Shipping Type Common Divisor
Air Cargo 5000
International Courier 6000
Freight Forwarder 4000
Custom User Defined

Frequently Asked Questions

What is volumetric weight?

Volumetric weight estimates shipping weight based on package size instead of actual mass.

Why is my chargeable weight higher?

Shipping companies charge whichever is greater between actual and volumetric weight.

Why do different couriers use different divisors?

Every courier has its own pricing policy. Some use 4000, 5000, or 6000.

Can I use a custom divisor?

Yes. Select "Custom" and enter the divisor provided by your courier.
